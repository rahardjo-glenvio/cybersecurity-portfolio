Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by going to Profile Settings and using the "Change Password" form. Enter your current password and choose a new one.
How do I transfer money?
Navigate to the Transfer Money page, select the recipient from the dropdown, enter the amount, and click "Send Money Now". The transfer is instant!
Is this a real banking application?
No! SecureBank is an intentionally vulnerable educational platform designed for penetration testing practice and cybersecurity learning. Never use it with real credentials or sensitive data.
What vulnerabilities can I practice?
SecureBank includes 10+ vulnerabilities: SQL Injection, IDOR, XSS, CSRF, Broken Authentication, Plain Text Passwords, Missing Input Validation, and more. Each vulnerability is intentional for educational purposes.
